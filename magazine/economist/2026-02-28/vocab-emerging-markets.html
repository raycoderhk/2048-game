🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This article discusses how emerging markets are experiencing renewed investor interest despite global economic uncertainties, with capital flows shifting towards developing economies.

中文：這篇文章討論了新興市場如何在全球經濟不確定性下重新吸引投資者興趣，資金流向轉向發展中經濟體。

emerging adj /ɪˈmɜː.dʒɪŋ/ Recently coming into existence; developing
adj. 新興的，發展中的
"Emerging markets offer growth potential"
譯：新興市場提供增長潛力
capital n /ˈkæp.ɪ.təl/ Wealth in the form of money or assets
n. 資本，資金
"Fresh capital flows into emerging economies"
譯：新資金流入新興經濟體
investment n /ɪnˈvest.mənt/ The action of investing money
n. 投資
"Foreign investment is increasing"
譯：外資投資正在增加
volatility n /ˌvɒl.əˈtɪl.ə.ti/ Liability to change rapidly; instability
n. 波動性，不穩定
"Market volatility concerns investors"
譯：市場波動令投資者擔憂
diversify v /daɪˈvɜː.sɪ.faɪ/ To vary or broaden the range of something
v. 多元化，分散
"Diversify investment portfolios"
譯：多元化投資組合
yield n /jiːld/ The full amount of profit or return
n. 收益，回報
"Higher yields attract investors"
譯：更高收益吸引投資者
currency n /ˈkʌr.ən.si/ A system of money in general use
n. 貨幣，通貨
"Currency fluctuations affect returns"
譯：貨幣波動影響回報
inflation n /ɪnˈfleɪ.ʃən/ A general increase in prices
n. 通貨膨脹
"Inflation rates vary across markets"
譯：通脹率在各市場有所不同
portfolio n /pɔːtˈfəʊ.li.əʊ/ A range of investments held by a person or organization
n. 投資組合
"Emerging market portfolio allocation"
譯：新興市場投資組合配置
liquidity n /lɪˈkwɪd.ə.ti/ The availability of liquid assets
n. 流動性
"Market liquidity is improving"
譯：市場流動性正在改善
