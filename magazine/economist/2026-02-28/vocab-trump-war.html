📰 Article:Donald Trump is at risk of launching a war without purpose
📂 Section:Leaders
📅 Date:February 28, 2026
📊 Level:Advanced (C1-C2)
🔗 Original:The Economist
🗄️ Archive:Archive.ph

🌍 Article Context | 文章背景

This Leaders article warns that President Trump's foreign policy lacks clear strategic objectives, risking military engagement without defined goals or exit strategies, potentially leading to prolonged conflicts.

中文：這篇領導者文章警告說，特朗普總統的外交政策缺乏明確的戰略目標，可能導致軍事介入而沒有定義明確的目標或撤離策略，可能導致長期衝突。

你的句子
purpose n /ˈpɜː.pəs/ The reason for which something is done
n. 目的，意圖
"A war without purpose is dangerous"
譯：沒有目的的戰爭是危險的
strategic adj /strəˈtiː.dʒɪk/ Relating to long-term planning
adj. 戰略的，策略的
"Clear strategic objectives are essential"
譯：明確的戰略目標至關重要
objective n /əbˈdʒek.tɪv/ A goal or aim; something worked toward
n. 目標，目的
"Military objectives must be clearly defined"
譯：軍事目標必須明確定義
engagement n /ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ.mənt/ Participation or involvement; military conflict
n. 參與，介入；戰鬥
"Military engagement without clear goals"
譯：沒有明確目標的軍事介入
prolonged adj /prəˈlɒŋd/ Continuing for a long time
adj. 長期的，延長的
"Prolonged conflicts drain resources"
譯：長期衝突消耗資源
intervention n /ˌɪn.təˈven.ʃən/ The action of becoming involved in something
n. 干預，介入
"Foreign military intervention raises questions"
譯：外國軍事干預引發質疑
consequence n /ˈkɒn.sɪ.kwəns/ A result or effect of an action
n. 後果，結果
"Unintended consequences of war"
譯：戰爭的意外後果
diplomacy n /dɪˈpləʊ.mə.si/ The practice of conducting negotiations between nations
n. 外交，外交手段
"Diplomacy should precede military action"
譯：外交應先於軍事行動
escalation n /ˌes.kəˈleɪ.ʃən/ A rapid increase in intensity
n. 升級，惡化
"Risk of conflict escalation"
譯：衝突升級的風險
withdrawal n /wɪðˈdrɔː.əl/ The action of withdrawing; removal
n. 撤軍，撤退
"A clear withdrawal strategy is needed"
譯：需要明確的撤離策略
