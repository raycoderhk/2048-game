🔌 About Wired

Wired (連線雜誌) is a monthly American magazine focusing on emerging technologies and their impact on culture, economy, and politics. Known for cutting-edge journalism and futuristic thinking.

適合學習者： 對科技、創新、未來趨勢感興趣的學習者。文章難度較高 (C1-C2)， 適合 IT 從業員、科技愛好者、創業家，或想學習科技英文的進階學習者。
