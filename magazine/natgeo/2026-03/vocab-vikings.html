🌍 The Vikings Who Vanished

Vocabulary & Quiz | 詞彙與測驗

📖 Read Original Article →

📝 Article Summary | 文章摘要

Around A.D. 1000, Vikings led by Erik the Red sailed to Greenland and established colonies that survived for 500 years before mysteriously vanishing in the mid-1400s. Archaeologists are now uncovering new theories about their disappearance, linking their fate to climate change and cultural adaptation.

公元 1000 年左右，由紅髮埃里克帶領的維京人航行到格陵蘭，建立了持續 500 年的殖民地，但在 15 世紀中期神秘消失。考古學家現在揭示了他們消失的新理論，將其命運與氣候變化同文化適應聯繫起來。

📝 Quiz | 小測驗

Choose the correct answer for each question. | 為每道題選擇正確答案。

1. What does "excavate" mean?

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Excavate" means to dig carefully, especially to uncover archaeological remains. In the article, archaeologist Michael Nielsen was excavating a Viking site in Greenland.
「Excavate」意思是小心挖掘，特別係為了發掘考古遺址。文章中，考古學家 Michael Nielsen 喺格陵蘭發掘維京遺址。

📚 Detailed Explanation | 詳細解釋

2. A "midden" is:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
A "midden" is an ancient trash heap where archaeologists often find artifacts. The wooden horse was found in a midden, raising questions about why it was discarded.
「Midden」係古代垃圾堆，考古學家常喺度發現文物。木馬喺垃圾堆中發現，引發點解佢被丟棄嘅疑問。

3. What is a "fjord"?

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
A "fjord" is a long, narrow sea inlet with steep cliffs, formed by glacial erosion. The Norse settlers lived in the serpentine fjords of southwestern Greenland.
「Fjord」係狹長嘅海灣，兩側係陡峭懸崖，由冰川侵蝕形成。諾爾斯定居者喺格陵蘭西南部蜿蜒嘅峽灣居住。

4. If a species is "extinct," it means:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Extinct" means a species has completely died out. Archaeologist Thomas McGovern suggests the Norse Greenlanders all went extinct—they didn't escape.
「Extinct」意思係物種完全滅絕。考古學家 Thomas McGovern 認為格陵蘭嘅諾爾斯人都滅絕了——佢哋冇逃脫。

5. "Relics" are:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Relics" are objects from the past, especially those with historical significance. The stone ruins were relics of the Norse people who didn't survive.
「Relics」係過去嘅物品，特別係有歷史意義嘅。石頭廢墟係未能倖存嘅諾爾斯人嘅遺物。

6. Erik the Red was an:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
Erik the Red was an outlaw—a convicted murderer exiled from Iceland. He "discovered" Greenland while serving his exile sentence.
紅髮埃里克係逃犯——因謀殺罪被流放冰島。佢喺服流放刑期間「發現」了格陵蘭。

7. "Exile" means:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Exile" is punishment where someone is barred from their homeland. Erik the Red discovered Greenland during his exile from Iceland for murder.
「Exile」係一種懲罰，禁止某人返回家園。紅髮埃里克喺因謀殺被流放冰島期間發現了格陵蘭。

8. An "apocalypse" is:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Apocalypse" means a catastrophic final event. If the Greenlanders died in an apocalypse (plague or war), Europeans apparently didn't notice.
「Apocalypse」意思係災難性嘅最終事件。如果格陵蘭人死於大災難（瘟疫或戰爭），歐洲人顯然冇注意到。

9. A "compelling" theory is:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Compelling" means powerfully convincing or irresistible. Archaeologists are assembling a compelling new theory linking the Norse fate to climate change.
「Compelling」意思係令人信服或無法抗拒嘅。考古學家正在匯集一個令人信服嘅新理論，將諾爾斯人嘅命運與氣候變化聯繫起來。

10. "Heathland" refers to:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Heathland" is open, uncultivated land with low shrubs like heather. The Norse carried their culture from the fjords and heathlands of Scandinavia to Greenland.
「Heathland」係開闊、未開墾嘅土地，生長著低矮灌木（如石楠）。諾爾斯人將他們嘅文化從斯堪嘅納維亞嘅峽灣同荒野帶到了格陵蘭。
