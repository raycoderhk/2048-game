🐀 The Naked Mole Rat

Vocabulary & Quiz | 詞彙與測驗

📖 Read Original Article →

📝 Article Summary | 文章摘要

The naked mole rat, a nearly blind rodent, can live for decades without showing signs of aging. Scientists like Rochelle Buffenstein study these creatures to unlock secrets of longevity, cancer resistance, and disease prevention that could benefit human health.

裸鼴鼠是一種幾乎失明的囓齒動物，可以存活數十年而不顯示衰老跡象。像 Rochelle Buffenstein 這樣的科學家研究這些生物，以解開長壽、抗癌同預防疾病的秘密，這些可能有益於人類健康。

📝 Quiz | 小測驗

Choose the correct answer for each question. | 為每道題選擇正確答案。

1. "Longevity" refers to:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Longevity" means long life or the length of time someone lives. The article discusses humanity's obsession with longevity and the $44 billion industry around it.
「Longevity」意思係長壽或壽命。文章討論人類對長壽嘅痴迷同圍繞佢嘅 440 億美元產業。

2. "Eusocial" animals:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Eusocial" means living in highly organized colonies with division of labor. Naked mole rats were the first known eusocial mammal, with queens and workers.
「Eusocial」意思係生活在高度組織化嘅群落中，有勞動分工。裸鼴鼠係已知第一種真社會性哺乳動物，有蟻后同工人。

3. "Diminishment" means:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Diminishment" means reduction or becoming less. The oldest naked mole rat showed no diminishment of muscle mass, bone density, or metabolism even at nearly 40.
「Diminishment」意思係減少或變少。最老嘅裸鼴鼠即使接近 40 歲，都冇顯示肌肉質量、骨密度或新陳代謝嘅減少。

4. "Vascular" relates to:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Vascular" relates to blood vessels that carry blood throughout the body. The naked mole rat's vascular function stayed strong even at advanced age.
「Vascular」同血管有關，血管將血液輸送到全身。裸鼴鼠嘅血管功能即使喺高齡仍然強勁。

5. "Neurodegeneration" is:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Neurodegeneration" is the progressive loss of neuron structure or function, seen in diseases like Alzheimer's. Naked mole rats show no signs of neurodegeneration.
「Neurodegeneration」係神經元結構或功能嘅逐漸喪失，見於阿爾茨海默病等疾病。裸鼴鼠冇顯示神經退化嘅跡象。

6. An "anesthetic" is used to:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
An "anesthetic" causes loss of sensation or pain. Naked mole rats can survive 18 minutes without oxygen, making anesthetics ineffective on them.
「Anesthetic」引起感覺或疼痛喪失。裸鼴鼠可以喺無氧情況下生存 18 分鐘，使麻醉劑對佢哋無效。

7. "Menopause" is:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Menopause" is when menstruation permanently stops. Female naked mole rats never go through menopause—they breed until death.
「Menopause」係月經永久停止。雌性裸鼴鼠從不經歷更年期——佢哋持續繁殖直到死亡。

8. "Cognitive" relates to:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Cognitive" relates to mental processes like thinking, understanding, and learning. Researchers seek to prevent cognitive decline in aging.
「Cognitive」同思維、理解同學習等心理過程有關。研究人員尋求防止衰老期間嘅認知能力下降。

9. A "colony" is:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
A "colony" is a group of organisms of the same species living together. Naked mole rat colonies have 2 to over 100 animals, each with a queen.
「Colony」係同一物種嘅生物群體生活在一起。裸鼴鼠群落有 2 到超過 100 隻動物，每個群落有一隻蟻后。

10. "Juvenile" means:

💡 Explanation | 解釋:
"Juvenile" means young or immature. Naked mole rats maintain juvenile biological features—even fetal ones—as they age.
「Juvenile」意思係年輕或不成熟。裸鼴鼠隨著年齡增長保持青少年嘅生物特徵——甚至胎兒時期嘅特徵。
