📸 About National Geographic

National Geographic (國家地理雜誌) is one of the world's most iconic science and exploration magazines. Known for its stunning photography, in-depth articles about nature, science, culture, and conservation.

適合學習者： 對自然、科學、地理、文化感興趣的學習者。文章難度中等 (B1-B2)，圖片豐富幫助理解， 適合家庭學習或學校教育用途。
